Our Mission This Year

We are a passionate group of students dedicated to creating a sustainable future for our school and community, with a major focus this year on achieving our Maryland Green School Recertification.

The Road to Recertification

The Maryland Green School certification is a prestigious award that recognizes our school's commitment to environmental education and sustainable practices. This year, our club is spearheading the effort to get recertified!

This involves documenting all our environmental projects, integrating sustainability into the curriculum, and promoting conservation throughout the school. Every project we do, from building birdhouses to our upcoming "No Idling" campaign, is a crucial step towards reaching our goal and making MRHS a leader in environmental stewardship.

Our Work & Impact

See some of the hands-on projects we're proud of.

Pollinator & Rain Garden

We designed and maintain a beautiful garden with native Maryland plants to support local pollinators and manage rainwater runoff effectively.

Birdhouse Installations

To support local bird populations, we built and installed several birdhouses around the school campus, providing safe nesting spots.

Storm Drain Stenciling

We raised awareness about water pollution by stenciling messages on storm drains, reminding everyone that they lead directly to our waterways.

Awareness Campaigns

Through yearly bulletin boards and posters, we educate the student body on key environmental issues and share tips for a greener lifestyle.
