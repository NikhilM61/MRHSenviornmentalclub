Our Mission & The Green School Certification

The Marriotts Ridge Environmental Club leads the charge in maintaining our school's prestigious Maryland Green School certification. This award recognizes our sustained commitment to environmental education, best practices, and community engagement.

We achieve this through hands-on projects, school-wide awareness campaigns, and consistent efforts to reduce our ecological footprint. From managing recycling programs to planting native gardens, every action we take contributes to a healthier school environment and reinforces the principles of the Green School program.

Our Past Projects

See the impact we've made through our hands-on work.

School-Wide Recycling Overhaul

We streamlined the school's recycling collection process, introduced clearer signage, and ran an educational campaign that significantly increased our recycling rates.

Native Pollinator Garden

Designed and planted a garden with native Maryland flowers to support local pollinators like bees and butterflies, creating a living laboratory for science classes.

Annual Earth Day Fair

Organized a fun and interactive fair for students and staff with games, informational booths, and guest speakers to celebrate Earth Day and promote green habits.
