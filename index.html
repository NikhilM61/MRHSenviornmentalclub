About Us

Our club is dedicated to promoting environmental awareness and sustainability within the Marriotts Ridge High School community. We work on various projects to help our school maintain its Green School certification. Our activities include recycling programs, energy conservation initiatives, and educational campaigns. This year, we are heavily focused on the Green School recertification process.

Past Projects

Native Pollinator and Rain Garden

We designed and created a native pollinator and rain garden to support local wildlife and manage stormwater runoff.

Birdhouses

We built and installed birdhouses around the school grounds to provide nesting sites for local bird species.

Storm Drain Stenciling

We stenciled storm drains to raise awareness about the connection between storm drains and our local waterways.

Environmental Awareness Bulletin Board

We create a yearly bulletin board to educate the school community about environmental issues.
